FAQ
How do I book an order with customfurnish.com?

Ans: You can book an order with us online by clicking on the BOOK NOW button and furnishing all the mandatory information & files


Is the booking amount refundable?

Ans: If you are opting for Design only package,then the amount you pay is design fee and it is not refundable.However if you select Design and Execute package,
the amount you pay at the time of booking the order will be absorbed in the total project cost.


Can I switch the package I select at later point?

Ans: You are free to switch from one package to other at any time.If you decide to switch from Design only to Design & Execute, your booking amount will be absorbed in
the total project cost. However, if you sign-up for Design & Execute package and later decide to avail our design services, we'll not be providing you the Designs,BOM &
Engineering drawings.
